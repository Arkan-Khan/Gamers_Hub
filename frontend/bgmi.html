BGMI

BGMI, short for Battlegrounds Mobile India, is a mobile-only battle royale game designed specifically for the Indian market. It takes the core gameplay of PUBG Mobile, where up to 100 players fight to be the last one standing, but with adjustments to meet local regulations. You scavenge for weapons, vehicles, and armor on a shrinking battlefield, with various other game modes like team deathmatch available for a change of pace. BGMI is free to play and allows customization of your character, making it a popular choice for mobile gamers in India seeking a thrilling online multiplayer experience.
